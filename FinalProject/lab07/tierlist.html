Reflection on Candy Tier List

Creating this candy tier list helped me but my favorite candies on a list and tier them from most liked to least liked. I love jolly ranchers the most and they would be my go to candy choice. Baby Ruths are my least liked but I would still eat them if I am in the mood for choclate.
